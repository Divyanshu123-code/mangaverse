Frequently Asked Questions

1 How Is SpinzyWheel Different from Other Spin the Wheel Tools?

SpinzyWheel focuses on simplicity, fairness, and customization. Unlike basic wheel spinners, it allows you to fully customize visuals, sounds, and spin behavior while keeping the experience fast and intuitive. Everything runs directly in your browser with no setup required.

2 What Can I Customize on a SpinzyWheel?

You can customize almost every part of your wheel, including item names, colors, fonts, background images, spin duration, and sound effects. You can also control whether results are removed after each spin, making it ideal for games, raffles, or task rotation.

3 When Should I Use a Spin the Wheel Tool?

A spin the wheel tool is useful whenever you need a fair and unbiased choice. It is commonly used for classroom activities, team decisions, giveaways, challenges, live streams, and casual games with friends or family.

4 Can I Save or Share My Custom Wheel?

Yes. SpinzyWheel allows you to save your custom wheels for later use and generate a shareable link that others can access instantly. This makes it easy to reuse wheels or share them with groups, teams, or event participants.

5 Is SpinzyWheel Truly Random and Safe to Use?

Each spin is generated using a random selection process designed to prevent bias or predictable patterns. SpinzyWheel does not track your wheel entries without permission, ensuring both fairness and privacy while you use the tool.

6 Do I Need to Sign Up or Pay to Use SpinzyWheel?

No registration or payment is required. SpinzyWheel is completely free to use and works instantly in your browser. You can start spinning right away without downloads, subscriptions, or hidden limitations.
